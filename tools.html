The Conversation Coach

AI-powered tools to explore how understood you feel

How It Works

📊

Self-Assessment

Answer 15 research-informed questions about your relationships, communication patterns, and sense of being understood. Get quantitative scores plus a personalized AI-generated analysis of your unique pattern.

🎙️

Voice Chat

Have a spoken conversation with an AI counselor about how understood you feel. Uses voice recognition and speech synthesis for a natural, hands-free experience. Best in Chrome.

🔒

Private & Secure

Your responses are processed in real-time and never stored. No account needed. No data collected. This is a tool for self-reflection, not data gathering.
